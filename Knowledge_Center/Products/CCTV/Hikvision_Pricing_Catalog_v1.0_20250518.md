## Pricing Methodology

- **Wholesale Cost**: Base cost from supplier
- **VAT**: 10% of wholesale cost
- **VAT-Inclusive Cost**: Wholesale Cost + VAT
- **Markup**: 35% of VAT-Inclusive Cost
- **Final Price**: VAT-Inclusive Cost + Markup (rounded up to nearest whole BHD)
